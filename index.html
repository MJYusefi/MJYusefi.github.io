Research Interests & Projects

My primary research focus is on the modernization of power grids through energy storage integration and intelligent planning.

Mobile Battery Energy Storage Systems (MBESS)

Investigating the economic feasibility and technical benefits of deploying mobile battery units in distribution networks. This includes routing optimization, sizing, and scheduling to enhance grid resilience during outages.

Microgrid Resilience & Optimization

Developing algorithms to optimize the operation of isolated and grid-connected microgrids, ensuring stability and minimizing operational costs using advanced optimization techniques.

Data-Driven Energy Management

Utilizing machine learning and data analytics to predict load patterns and renewable generation, improving the decision-making process for grid operators.
